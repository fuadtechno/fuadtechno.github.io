Chapter 01

The Architecture of Connectivity

Designing a network is about balancing Business Goals with Technical Constraints. It begins with understanding the lifecycle of a project.

Design Goals

  • Scalability: Supporting growth without redesigning the core.

  • Availability: Achieving "Five Nines" (99.999%) uptime.

  • Manageability: Simplifying O&M with SDN and automation.

PPDIOO Methodology

Prepare: High-level strategy & vision.

Plan: Auditing existing infrastructure.

Design: Logical & Physical diagrams.

Implement: Deployment of new assets.

Operate: Day-to-day network health.

Optimize: Proactive performance tuning.

Access Control Lists (ACL)

Standard ACLs (1-99) filter by Source IP only. Extended ACLs (100-199) filter by Source/Dest IP and Port numbers.
